Destination: Varkala

Varkala is a beautiful scenic location situated in Kerala. Most visitors look forward to embracing with the blissful back waters and spending time amongst nature.

Travel Tips:

  • Make sure to wear caps and sunscreen
  • Pack comfortable clothes for activities such as boating and trekking
